How to use tages

2000-01-01 2 min read # How to use tags

I tagged the articles differently according to its content, creator, form and theme. According to these tags, it can quickly find the relevant articles.

By Content

日志 | journal : What I have seen, heard and thought recently
学习 | study : Notes and thoughts on learning new skills
研究 | research : Research progress and expansion related to my major
影音 | entertainment : Sharing of movies, TV series, games and music
阅读 | reading : Notes of reading that are not related to my major
游记 | travel : Travel news and tourism strategy
生活 | life : Fragments of life not in the above categories

By Creator

原创 | original : I wrote it by myself, but there may be a little quotation.
转载 | reproduction : It's completely from others, I just share it for worth.
综述 | review : Mainly based on other's, but I made reviews or comments.

By Form

文字 | text : Text based article, the main and the most form.
图片 | image : Image based article, photography or painting.
视频 | video : Video based article, may connect to other websites.

By theme

In addition to the above tags, I have also added different theme tags to some articles, such as architecture, art, literature, philosophy, etc., which are mainly used to record my reading and learning, so it can also be selected to search relevant articles.
